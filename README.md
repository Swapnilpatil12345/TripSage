
# AI-Powered Virtual Travel Guide 

## Overview
This project generates AI-powered travel itineraries based on user preferences like destination, budget, duration, and activities.

### 🏞️ Sample Travel Itinerary - Kolhapur

#### ✈️ Trip Details:
- **Destination:** Kolhapur
- **Duration:** 2 Days
- **Budget:** INR 2800 (approx.)
- **Activities:** Sightseeing, Hiking, Water Adventure
- **Trip Type:** Cultural & Adventure

📍 Day 1: Cultural Exploration & Adventure

🌅 Morning:

Arrive in Kolhapur and check into Kolhapur Residency (Budget: INR 500/night).

Visit Bhavani Mandap, a historical temple dedicated to Goddess Bhavani.

Explore the New Palace, a majestic 17th-century structure built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

🎢 Afternoon:

Embark on a white-water rafting adventure on the Panchganga River (Approx. INR 300/person).

Enjoy lunch at Dehati, known for its traditional Maharashtrian cuisine.

🌆 Evening:

Stroll through local markets for Kolhapuri chappals & jaggery shopping.

Savor an authentic Maharashtrian Thali for dinner at Dehati Restaurant.

🏕️ Day 2: Nature & Thrill

🌄 Morning:

Hike to Rankala Lake, a scenic spot perfect for photography and relaxation.

Seek blessings at Mahalaxmi Temple, a revered Hindu shrine.

🎯 Afternoon:

Experience the thrill of zip-lining at Velocity Entertainmentz (Approx. INR 500/person).

Visit Jyotiba Temple, a famous pilgrimage site atop a hill.

🌅 Evening:

Enjoy a sunset boat ride on the Panchganga River (Approx. INR 200/person).

Wrap up your trip with dinner at Hotel Sayaji, renowned for its fine dining and city views.

🏨 Accommodation Options:

Kolhapur Residency: Budget-friendly with a central location.

Hotel Sayaji: Luxury stay with a rooftop restaurant.

🍽️ Dining Recommendations:

Dehati: Traditional Maharashtrian flavors in a rustic ambiance.

Hotel Sayaji: Premium multi-cuisine dining with a panoramic cityscape.

📌 Travel Tips:

Pre-book adventure activities like rafting and zip-lining to avoid last-minute issues.

Wear comfortable hiking shoes and carry sunscreen for outdoor activities.

Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Kolhapur’s cuisine is spicy, so be prepared for a flavorful experience!

🚀 How It Works:

This itinerary was generated using an AI-based travel planner. The model takes in user preferences and generates a personalized travel plan.

🛠️ Tech Stack:

Python (Streamlit for UI)

Google Gemini AI (for itinerary generation)

dotenv (for API key management)
